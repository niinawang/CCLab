ABOUT COLORBURST

The generative art produced at the end is based on the user's input to the questions. Every number corresponds to a color. The lower numbers represent warmer colors to symbolize one's personaility to be more outgoing and extroverted, while the higher numbers correspond to darker colors to symbolize one 's personality to be less outgoing and more introverted. The generative art tries to replicate the dendrites and the neurons within the brain, ultimately showcasing an indivdual internaally through various colors.
